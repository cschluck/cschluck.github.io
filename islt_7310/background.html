Experience

Since the fall of 2016 Cindy has been a business teacher in the Independence School District. Currently, she facilitates learning at two student operated businesses with juniors and seniors through an entrepreneurship capstone course. Through this experience students embrace the entrepreneurial spirit through real businesses and gain key entrepreneurial skills such as critical thinking, problem-solving, communication, risk-taking, and teamwork. So far in her career this is her favorite position. It has taken all of her past experiences and passions and molded them together into one position. In her position with ISD, Cindy has the opportunity to use her past experience to help empower students to be more entrepreneurial.

Certifications

While working for New Franklin R-1 Cindy became a certified eMINTS teacher and an Affilate eMINTS trainer. An Affiliate Trainer is certified to present eMINTS curriculum to professionals in order for them to get eMINTS credentials. The eMINTS National Center helps educators focus on high qualitiy, inquiry based lesson design while implementing technology. This certification comes with high quality professional development that enhances classrooms across the world.
